Sports

Sports are organized physical or mental activities that involve skill, competition, and rules, typically undertaken for recreation, exercise, or professional purposes.

About Sports In india

Sports in India encompass a rich blend of ancient indigenous games like Kabaddi and Kho-Kho, and modern sports such as cricket (the nation's most popular sport), along with significant success in field hockey. The British colonial era introduced sports like football and tennis, while sports like chess have ancient Indian origins. India has a developing sports infrastructure, with professional leagues in various sports and a growing focus on fostering a diverse sports culture beyond just cricket.
