About Us

We are taking classes for 10th, 11th and 12th std students. We provide coaching for all subjects. We have well experienced teachers. We also provide online classes for students who are not able to attend offline classes. We provide study materials and notes for all subjects. We also conduct weekly tests and monthly exams to evaluate the students performance. We also provide doubt clearing sessions for students who are facing difficulties in understanding the concepts. We also provide special classes for competitive exams like NEET, JEE, etc. We also provide career guidance for students who are confused about their future. We also provide personality development classes to improve the overall personality of the students. We also provide extracurricular activities like sports, arts, etc to enhance the overall development of the students. We also provide hostel facilities for outstation students. We also provide transport facilities for local students. We also provide scholarships for meritorious students. We also provide flexible timings for working students. We also provide affordable fees structure for all students. We also provide a friendly and comfortable environment for students to learn and grow.
